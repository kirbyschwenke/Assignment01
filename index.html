I have always been someone that thrives in an environment that fosters creativity and analytical thinking, and the IT industry is an industry that combines these areas of my life. Project Idea: No Waste Gardening Community App

Overview

The No Waste Gardening Community will be an app that relies on user-generated content to form a database of ideas to reduce waste through gardening, troubleshoot any issues as well as connect with like-minded individuals. Currently there are a number of forums and articles in this area, however, there is no one-stop-shop for ideas that is also interactive so users can eventually receive quick, personalized assistance and this is what this community app hopes to provide.

Motivation

‘Almost 1 million Australians have lost their jobs since social-distancing measures’ (Janda 2020) came into effect, as a result of which we saw Aussie’s buying out puzzle stores, flocking to parks and ultimately looking for hobbies. We also saw mental health issues on the rise with a recent survey by Professor Fisher at Monash University (Scott 2020) that found 25% of people were experiencing moderate to severe symptoms of depression. Gardening is both a great, affordable hobby and a great way to clear one’s mind, in a community form we would also be creating another way for individuals to connect. These points coupled with a greater demand for sustainable practices motivates the idea of a No Waste Gardening Community.

Description

The No Waste Gardening Community app would be split into a few sections;

Information – the information section would work as a forum and be where the bulk of user-generated content is stored. As part of the submission of content users will be required to answer a set of questions to assist with filtering the content into suitable pages and menus. This content will then be displayed in a number of menu’s that are easily navigated by the user. Content in this area could be anything from “how to grow capsicum from food scraps” to “ how to use egg shells to fertilize your garden”.

Help – this section would be an area where users can post comments and questions on issues they may having, this will have the ability for other users to comment back and provide advice as well as attach additional files to support text.

News Feed – this section will be the success stories and a great place to promote the information section. It would work similarly to a Facebook newsfeed that is consistently updated.

Your Profile – the ability to provide information about yourself and displays all previous posts in the above areas.

Other features that would be included are, social media integration and the ability to like and dislike posts to allow for posts that suit the users needs to pop up first.

Tools and Technologies

The main tool required would be an app development software that can be used on both Android and Apple. This will rely on technologies such as AJAX, to ensure that content is consistently updated and improve the search function as well as Javascript to ensure a dynamic, user-centred app.

Skills Required

In order to complete this assignment I would be required to learn more about HTML, CSS and Javascript to ensure a aesthetically pleasing functional app. I would also need to learn about the guidelines for submitting apps to the app store and play store. I would also be required to use the Adobe Creative Cloud to create aesthetically pleasing content.

Outcome

The hope with the No Waste Gardening Community App is to create a one-stop-shop for all your gardening needs and hopes to draw more people to use vegetable scraps to garden and reduce overall household waste. It is hoped that creating easy to understand content will allow for the community to grow, with more users wanting to take up Gardening to ease mental stress and reduce waste.
